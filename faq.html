Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to get started renting?

It's a fairly simple process, but you will need to keep in mind a few things so it all runs smoothly:

  • You must be 18 years or older (or accompanied by a parent or guardian) to rent
  • A valid ID or credit card
  • We do not accept reservations and all rentals are first-come, first-serve
What if something breaks while I'm renting it?

Not to worry. We know part of exploring the outdoors is that it's tough on gear. Normal wear and tear is expected and you there's no penalty for that. Product defects and broken fasteners and the like are also part of that normal wear and tear. Intentional harm to products, however, will incure a penalty. This is will depend on the severity.

What if I can't get back to return items during business hours?

Don't worry! Sometimes it's tough to get back into town at the time you were expecting. We have late-night drop off boxes set up outside our store for anytime before or after business hours. However, if we are open, we ask that you come inside to return gear.
