Script 1

You are fairly new to the area and you are looking for a map and trail information for Multonomah Falls.

What we're testing:

  • That site and page navigation is clear and usable
  • Images and maps are functioning correctly
  • That site content in clear, and useful

Demographic

Males and females, ages 18 - 50, high school education or above, income level $15,000-$75,000

Script 2

You are looking for a list of great hikes in the surrounding Portland area. You aren't sure where you want to go yet, so you are looking for a good variety to pick from. Find a page that meets these requirments.

What we're testing:

  • That site and page navigation is clear and usable
  • Quality of content

Demographic

Males and females, ages 24 - 50, college education, income level $15,000-$60,000

Script 3

You are new to backpacking and are looking for some basic information like what to bring with you and some packing essentials. Navigate to the page where that can be found.

What we're testing:

  • That the navigation is clear and easy to use
  • That the link text makes sense for what's on the page

Demographic

Males and females (about a 40%:60% ratio), ages 24 - 50, college education or currently attending college, income level $15,000-$40,000
